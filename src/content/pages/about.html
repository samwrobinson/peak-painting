Peak Painting’s Mission

At Peak Painting, we transform homes and businesses across Vermont with professional interior and exterior painting services. Guided by integrity, transparency, and reliability, we’ve built a company that people trust and love.

What began as a shared vision between two brothers has become a business rooted in Vermont’s vibrant community. After years of experience in tech and painting, we saw an opportunity to offer something different: reliable, high-quality painting services that homeowners can count on. Vermont’s harsh weather can take a toll on properties, but with the right care, we believe every home can be as beautiful as the scenery surrounding it.
