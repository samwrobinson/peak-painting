Exterior House Painting Services

At Peak Painting, we believe your home’s exterior should be as stunning and welcoming as the Vermont landscapes that surround it. Our premium exterior painting services are designed to not only enhance your home’s curb appeal but also protect it from Vermont’s ever-changing weather conditions.

We understand that every home is unique. That’s why we take the time to assess your property’s needs, whether it’s combating fading paint, repairing worn surfaces, or selecting the perfect colors to complement your surroundings. With a commitment to quality craftsmanship and meticulous attention to detail, we deliver results that stand the test of time.

The Peak Painting Process

Our exterior painting services follow a streamlined process designed to ensure your complete satisfaction:

  1. Initial Consultation: We meet with you to understand your vision, preferences, and any specific challenges your home may face.
  2. Concept and Design: From power washing to repairing damaged siding, we ensure every surface is ready for a flawless finish.
  3. Color Selection: Collaborate with our experts to choose colors that highlight your home’s character and beauty.
  4. Execution: Our experienced team applies high-quality, weather-resistant paints for a smooth, professional finish.
  5. Completion: We complete a detailed inspection with you to ensure everything meets your expectations.
